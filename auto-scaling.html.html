Your Blueprint for Success

Creating Your First Plan

  • 1. Define Workload: Identify the application component that needs to scale (e.g., web servers).
  • 2. Create Launch Template: Build the blueprint for your instances (AMI, type, security).
  • 3. Configure ASG: Set min, max, and desired capacity across multiple Availability Zones.
  • 4. Attach Load Balancer: Distribute traffic evenly across your instances.
  • 5. Set Scaling Policies: Define the rules for scaling out and in (e.g., target CPU at 60%).
  • 6. Monitor & Adjust: Use CloudWatch to observe performance and fine-tune your policies.

Maintaining Your Plan

  • ‚úì Regularly Monitor: Review CloudWatch alarms and scaling history to ensure correct behavior.
  • ‚úì Update Launch Templates: Keep your AMIs and software up-to-date with security patches.
  • ‚úì Review Policies: As traffic patterns change, adjust your target values or schedules.
  • ‚úì Perform Load Tests: Periodically test your setup to verify it scales correctly under stress.
  • ‚úì Optimize Costs: Consider Spot Instances for non-critical workloads and Savings Plans for baseline capacity.
